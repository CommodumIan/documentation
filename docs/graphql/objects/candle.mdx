Candle stick representation of trading

```graphql
type Candle {
  timestamp: String!
  datetime: String!
  high: String!
  low: String!
  open: String!
  close: String!
  volume: String!
  interval: Interval!
}
```

### Fields

#### `timestamp` ([`String!`](/docs/graphql/scalars/string))

Unix epoch+nanoseconds for when the candle occurred

#### `datetime` ([`String!`](/docs/graphql/scalars/string))

RFC3339Nano formatted date and time for the candle

#### `high` ([`String!`](/docs/graphql/scalars/string))

High price (uint64)

#### `low` ([`String!`](/docs/graphql/scalars/string))

Low price (uint64)

#### `open` ([`String!`](/docs/graphql/scalars/string))

Open price (uint64)

#### `close` ([`String!`](/docs/graphql/scalars/string))

Close price (uint64)

#### `volume` ([`String!`](/docs/graphql/scalars/string))

Volume price (uint64)

#### `interval` ([`Interval!`](/docs/graphql/enums/interval))

Interval price (string)
