id: event
title: Event
---

union type for wrapped events in stream PROPOSAL is mapped to governance data, something to keep in mind

```graphql
union Event =
    TimeUpdate
  | MarketEvent
  | TransferResponses
  | PositionResolution
  | Order
  | Trade
  | Account
  | Party
  | MarginLevels
  | Proposal
  | Vote
  | MarketData
  | NodeSignature
  | LossSocialization
  | SettlePosition
  | Market
  | Asset
  | MarketTick
  | SettleDistressed
  | AuctionEvent
  | RiskFactor
  | Deposit
  | Withdrawal
  | OracleSpec
  | LiquidityProvision
```

### Possible types

#### [`TimeUpdate`](/graphql/objects/time-update)

#### [`MarketEvent`](/graphql/objects/market-event)

#### [`TransferResponses`](/graphql/objects/transfer-responses)

#### [`PositionResolution`](/graphql/objects/position-resolution)

#### [`Order`](/graphql/objects/order)

An order in Vega, if active it will be on the OrderBook for the market

#### [`Trade`](/graphql/objects/trade)

A trade on Vega, the result of two orders being 'matched' in the market

#### [`Account`](/graphql/objects/account)

An account record

#### [`Party`](/graphql/objects/party)

Represents a party on Vega, could be an ethereum wallet address in the future

#### [`MarginLevels`](/graphql/objects/margin-levels)

Margins for a given a trader

#### [`Proposal`](/graphql/objects/proposal)

#### [`Vote`](/graphql/objects/vote)

#### [`MarketData`](/graphql/objects/market-data)

Live data of a Market

#### [`NodeSignature`](/graphql/objects/node-signature)

Represents a signature for the approval of a resource from a validator

#### [`LossSocialization`](/graphql/objects/loss-socialization)

#### [`SettlePosition`](/graphql/objects/settle-position)

#### [`Market`](/graphql/objects/market)

Represents a product & associated parameters that can be traded on Vega, has an associated OrderBook and Trade history

#### [`Asset`](/graphql/objects/asset)

Represents an asset in vega

#### [`MarketTick`](/graphql/objects/market-tick)

#### [`SettleDistressed`](/graphql/objects/settle-distressed)

#### [`AuctionEvent`](/graphql/objects/auction-event)

#### [`RiskFactor`](/graphql/objects/risk-factor)

A risk factor emitted by the risk model for a given market

#### [`Deposit`](/graphql/objects/deposit)

The details of a deposit processed by vega

#### [`Withdrawal`](/graphql/objects/withdrawal)

The details of a withdrawal processed by vega

#### [`OracleSpec`](/graphql/objects/oracle-spec)

An oracle spec describe the oracle data that a product (or a risk model)
wants to get from the oracle engine.

#### [`LiquidityProvision`](/graphql/objects/liquidity-provision)

The command to be sent to the chain for a liquidity provision submission
